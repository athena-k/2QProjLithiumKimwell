Genshin Impact has been quite a popular game for the past year. It's an open-world game, focused on action and adventure. But what about the food?

While there are enemies and various structures in this fictional world, there are also many key resources to be found. There are precious stones, loot chests, and most importantly, food ingredients! We can hunt boars, birds and fish for meat, and use plants like berries and flowers as well. We can even buy ingredients like tofu and rice from shopkeepers in different nations! These are then carefully cooked to have different effects, not to mention that they look delicious and so very aesthetically pleasing! There was even a recent event all about cooking, so I'd like to talk a little more about the food!
let's get down to business~

There are common foods, expensive foods, and foods that can only be made from local specialties from the three currently released regions. While we can't physically experience eating them outselves, it would still be nice to appreciate them better, since a lot of players are more invested in the lore, the characters, and sacrificing their life's savings to the gacha system. :)

General
Food in this game isn't just a cosmetic item; they have their uses and fit under specific categories depending on their characteristics. Here are the basics!
Mondstadt
This region, inspired by Germany, is the City of the Wind. As such, their dishes reflect the free personalities of their citizens.
Liyue
This China-inspired land is the most commerically successful nation of the seven. Their dishes are mostly sophisticated and reflect their ideals clearly.
Inazuma
Based on real-life Japan, this region strives for eternity. Its dishes clearly showcase the culture and aesthetics that would be eternally recognizable around the world.
